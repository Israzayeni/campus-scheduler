## ID formats
- Courses:      gl3_<topic>       e.g. gl3_algo, gl3_db, gl3_os
- Rooms:        r<number>         e.g. r101, r201, r302
- Buildings:    b_<name>          e.g. b_main, b_tech, b_annex
- Slots:        s<number>         e.g. s1, s2 ... s40  (no gaps)
- Groups:       g<number>         e.g. g1, g2, g3, g4
- Instructors:  prof_<lastname>   e.g. prof_ali, prof_sana
